About This Site


In 2019, I started my bat mitzvah studies as an adult learner. When the pandemic forced my class to switch over to remote learning, it was challenging for me not to have the sense of reviewing a shared chanting document with my Rabbi. I started exploring the capabilities of Sefaria and came across the highlighting tool option and the ability to add mp3 audio clips. I was eager to share the possibilities with my classmates and created an 18 page instruction guide for how to add highlighting to text and how to find and insert relevant audio files. I'm not sure anyone took the time to wade through 18 pages of instructions and even I, in the end, was not motivated to create these types of study sheets for myself. It was simply too much effort.

When the 2021 Sefaria programming contest was announced, I decided to revisit my idea and explore the Sefaria API. I realized that by using the Sefaria API I could help automate the tedious steps in creating a Torah chanting source sheet. In a way, this is my bat mitzvah project. I hope that future students and others who want to chant Torah will find these Torah Chanting Source Sheets helpful in their learning process.

Yasher Koach!
