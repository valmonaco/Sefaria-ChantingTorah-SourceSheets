Welcome! If you are preparing to chant Torah, we've got something for you. The form on this page will bring together Torah text and audio on a customized Sefaria source sheet. This is only possible through the power of Sefaria and the amazing work of Cantor Wendy Shermet (Temple Israel, Omaha NE). If you find these free resources helpful, please consider expressing your appreciation through a donation to Sefaria and the Cantor Shermet Music Fund at Temple Israel.

A few caveats: The current trope cantillation (Binder/Schleifer) is from the Reform Judaism tradition (although including additional cantillation styles is on roadmap). The number of verses that can be added to a single sheet is capped at four. Please indicate if the ending verse is the end of an aliyah.
